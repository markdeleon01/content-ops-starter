      **Effective Date:  October 1, 2025**  

      SmylSync (“SmylSync,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of the data entrusted to us by our clients and their patients. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, retain, and protect information in connection with our services.

      ---
      
      **1. Information We Collect**
      
      SmylSync may collect the following types of information in the course of providing services:
      
      - Practice Information – Contact details, staff information, schedules, billing preferences, and other administrative data.
      
      - Patient Information (Protected Health Information/PHI) – Names, contact details, insurance data, treatment history, claims details, and other data necessary for practice operations.
      
      - Operational Data – Communication records, usage logs, and other administrative details shared in the course of service delivery.

      ---

      **2. How We Use Information**
      
      Information is used solely for the purpose of delivering contracted services, including but not limited to:
      
      - Processing and submitting insurance claims
      - Managing credentialing applications
      - Scheduling and patient coordination
      - Billing and insurance verification
      - Aging claims recovery and reporting
      - Other administrative tasks as agreed with the Client
      - We do not sell, rent, or share information for marketing purposes.

      ---

      **3. Data Retention**
      
      - Data is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill service obligations or comply with legal/regulatory requirements.
      
      - Upon termination of services, Clients may request the secure return or deletion of data.
      
      - SmylSync maintains secure deletion protocols when disposing of sensitive information.

      ---

      **4. Data Security**
      
      - All data is stored, accessed, and transmitted using HIPAA-compliant processes and tools.
      
      - Access to PHI and practice information is restricted to authorized personnel only.
      
      - Security safeguards include encryption, secure logins, access controls, and routine audits.

      ---

      **5. Client Responsibilities**
      
      - Clients are responsible for ensuring that any data shared with SmylSync is accurate and lawfully obtained.
      
      - Clients must notify SmylSync promptly of any unauthorized use of their systems or data that may affect service delivery.

      ---

      **6. Sharing of Information**
      
      SmylSync does not disclose Client or Patient information except:

      - To authorized SmylSync personnel delivering services
      - When required by law, regulation, or legal process
      - To comply with HIPAA and related healthcare obligations

      ---

      **7. International Data Transfer**
      
      Since SmylSync operates through Filipino professionals servicing U.S. dental practices, data may be transferred internationally. All such transfers are protected through confidentiality agreements, HIPAA training, and secure handling protocols.

      ---

      **8. Cookies & Technology**
      
      If Clients use SmylSync’s future technology platform or application, standard digital tools (e.g., cookies, session logs) may be used to enhance functionality. These tools will not be used to track patients or for marketing purposes.

      ---

      **9. Your Rights**
      
      Clients have the right to:
      
      - Request access to data held by SmylSync
      - Request correction or deletion of data (subject to legal requirements)
      - Request details on how SmylSync secures and uses their information

      ---

      **10. Updates to This Policy**
      
      We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect operational, legal, or regulatory changes. Updates will be communicated to Clients in advance.

      ---

      **11. Contact Us**
      
      For questions, requests, or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact:
      
      **[`info@smylsync.com`](/contact-us)**
